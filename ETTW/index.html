Escape To The West

Three Homes To Choose From

Unique and Individual

Experience Connemara Like A Local

Doon Hill House

Doon Hill House is located at the foot of the historic Doon Hill, in Ballyconneely. If you have a passion for history and discovery, this house might be the place for you to escape to the west.

Bijou Cottage

Bijou Cottage is conveniently set in the idylic village of Roundstone. This house suits those who enjoy lively pubs, short walks to the beach and cosy nights in beside the fire.

Ballyconneely House

Do you like golf, beaches and beautiful scenary? Ballyconneely House ticks all those boxes in abundance, providing a perfect base for those who want to explore.

Immerse Yourself

Ballyconneely, Roundstone and neighbouring town Clifden are all teeming with things to do all year round. Be it enjoy a pint of Guiness in the famous O'Dowds or play a round of golf on the reknowned Connemara Links, there is something to do it this beautiful part of the country

Tourist Destinations

Connemara is soaked in a wide range of diverse activities and atractions that all ages can enjoy. From historical sites that are dotted across the rugged country side to experiencing the mystical nature of the Wild Atlantic Way, you won't be short of things to do.

Live Music & Night Life

Music is embodied in the veins of the Irish. It won't take you too long to stumble into a bar that is in the midst of a full blown session and for you to be lost in the musicians etheral prowess.

Outdoor Activities & Sports

When it comes to outdoor activities it's a case of what can't you do rather what you can. The area surronding Clifden, Roundstone and Ballyconneely have so much to offer such as golf, surfing, beaches, horse riding and much more.

Shopping

You aren't going to find many high street shops in any of Connemara's towns but what you will find is bespoke, traditionally made cloths that are authentic to their core.
